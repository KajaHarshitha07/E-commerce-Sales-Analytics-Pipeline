# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Pipeline
> Analyzing 96K+ orders from Brazilian e-commerce platform Olist

## Problem Statement
Identify revenue trends, delivery bottlenecks, and customer segments
to help operations and marketing teams make data-driven decisions.

## Tools Used
Python | Pandas | SQLite | Scikit-learn | Power BI | Streamlit

## Key Findings
- 8.5% of deliveries are late — costing est. R$2.3M in refund exposure
- Health & Beauty is top revenue category (R$1.6M)
- 4 customer segments identified: Champions, Loyal, At Risk, Lost
- Logistic Regression predicts late delivery with 82% accuracy
